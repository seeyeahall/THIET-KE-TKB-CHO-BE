Kid Adventure Planner - Project Overview
Ten du an
Kid Adventure Planner

Muc tieu
Xay dung ung dung tieng Viet danh cho tre em tu 6 den 10 tuoi. Ung dung giup be tu thiet ke thoi khoa bieu ngay, tuan va thang thong qua trai nghiem phieu luu, kham pha, hinh anh, am thanh, phan thuong va AI dong hanh.

Day khong phai la ung dung quan ly thoi gian. Thoi khoa bieu chi la cong cu de be tu tao cuoc phieu luu moi ngay.

Nguoi dung
Tre em 6 den 10 tuoi.
Phu huynh quan ly tai khoan, ho so be, noi dung, AI provider va bao cao.
Admin he thong quan ly thu vien hoat dong, AI provider, storage va cau hinh.
Nguyen tac san pham
Be nhin thay vui truoc khi doc thay nhieu chu.
Giao dien it chu, nhieu hinh anh, thao tac cham/keo tha.
Frontend la trung tam trai nghiem.
Backend don gian, on dinh, phuc vu du lieu va AI context.
Moi noi dung quan trong phai luu trong database, khong hardcode.
AI phai hieu tung be, khong tra loi nhu chatbot chung chung.
Cong nghe chinh
Frontend: NextJS, TypeScript, Tailwind, PWA.
Backend: Python FastAPI.
Database: Supabase Postgres.
Auth: Supabase Auth.
Storage: Supabase Storage ket hop Cloudflare CDN/cache.
Hosting frontend: Cloudflare Pages.
Hosting backend: VPS, Render, Railway, Fly.io hoac Cloud Run.
AI providers: OpenAI, Gemini, Claude, OpenRouter, Ollama hoac API OpenAI-compatible.
