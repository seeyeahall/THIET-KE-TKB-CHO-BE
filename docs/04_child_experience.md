# Child Experience

## Nguyen tac trai nghiem cho be

- Be khong can doc nhieu van ban.
- Moi hanh dong phai co phan hoi truc quan.
- Lich la ban do phieu luu.
- Hoat dong phai gan voi doi song that.
- Phan thuong nen khuyen khich kham pha, khong gay ap luc.

## Ho so be

Moi be co:

- Ten.
- Tuoi.
- Avatar.
- Mau yeu thich.
- Con vat yeu thich.
- Chu de yeu thich.
- Hoat dong thich/khong thich.
- Ghi chu cua phu huynh.

## Trang chu cua be

Trang chu can tra loi nhanh:

- Hom nay be la ai?
- Hom nay chu de gi?
- Bay gio lam gi?
- Lam xong duoc gi?
- Neu chan thi hoi ai?

## Hanh vi than thien

- Loi chuc ngan khi be hoan thanh hoat dong.
- Goi y hoat dong tiep theo dua tren so thich.
- Neu be bo qua hoat dong, khong phat hay che trach.
- Neu be hoan thanh lien tiep, mo huy hieu nho.

- Gia dinh.

## An toan noi dung

- AI khong hoi thong tin nhay cam.
- AI khong dua loi khuyen y te/phap ly nguy hiem.
- AI khong khuyen be lam viec can nguoi lon giam sat neu khong ghi ro.
- Hoat dong co vat dung nguy hiem phai gan co "can phu huynh".
