# UI/UX Design

## Nguyen tac giao dien

- Frontend la trung tam.
- Be thao tac bang hinh anh, card lon, nut ro rang.
- It chu, uu tien bieu tuong, mau sac va anh minh hoa.
- Khong tao cam giac dashboard doanh nghiep.
- Thoi khoa bieu can giong ban do phieu luu hon la bang cong viec.

## Man hinh chinh

### Chon nguoi choi

Muc tieu: be chon ho so cua minh that nhanh.

Thanh phan:

- Logo/ten ung dung.
- Danh sach avatar be.
- Nut them be.
- Nut phu huynh.

### Trang chu cua be

Muc tieu: be thay ngay hom nay co gi vui.

Thanh phan:

- Avatar va ten be.
- Chu de hom nay.
- Lich hom nay.
- Nut vao thiet ke lich.
- Nut vao tro choi/kham pha.
- Nut AI dong hanh.
- Tien do xu, XP, huy hieu.

### Thiet ke lich

Muc tieu: be keo tha hoat dong vao khung gio/ngay.

Thanh phan:

- Thanh chon ngay trong tuan.
- Cot thoi gian.
- Card hoat dong co anh, ten, thoi luong.
- Nut them hoat dong.
- Nut AI tao lich.
- Nut luu.

### Thu vien hoat dong

Muc tieu: be/phu huynh tim hoat dong theo chu de.

Thanh phan:

- Filter chu de.
- Filter do tuoi.
- Filter thoi luong.
- Card hoat dong.
- Chi tiet hoat dong.

### AI dong hanh

Muc tieu: tro chuyen va xin goi y theo context.

Thanh phan:

- Khung chat.
- Goi y cau hoi nhanh.
- Nut tao lich tuan.
- Nut tao hoat dong moi.
- Trang thai dang doc context.

### Phu huynh

Muc tieu: quan ly noi dung va an toan.

Thanh phan:

- Danh sach be.
- Cau hinh AI.
- Thu vien hoat dong.
- Lich tuan.
- Bao cao don gian.
- Quan ly storage/media.

## Mau sac va cam giac

Nen dung bang mau tuoi sang nhung khong don dieu:

- Xanh la cho thien nhien.
- Vang cho nang luong.
- Xanh duong cho kham pha.
- Do/cam nhe cho phan thuong.
- Tim chi dung lam diem nhan, khong lam mau chu dao.

## Responsive

- Mobile-first cho tre em dung tablet/dien thoai.
- Desktop phuc vu phu huynh/admin.
- Card, nut va vung cham phai lon.
- Khong de chu tran card.
